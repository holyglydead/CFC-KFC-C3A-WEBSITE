Brief History of KFC

CFC Kids for Christ (KFC) is one of the dynamic family ministries under Couples for Christ (CFC), a global Catholic movement for the renewal and strengthening of Christian family life. KFC was founded in 1993 in Manila, Philippines, with a clear mission of evangelizing children aged 4 to 12 years old.

KFC emphasizes the role of the family as the primary foundation of a child's faith journey, working closely with parents to foster strong Christian values in their children. Through interactive and fun activities such as bible storytelling, praise and worship, and values formation programs, KFC provides a nurturing space for kids to experience God's love and begin their spiritual growth.

The ministry has since grown beyond Manila and expanded globally, reaching thousands of children across many countries. By participating in camps, conferences, and various KFC programs, children not only develop a deep connection with God but also build lifelong friendships with peers who share the same Christian values.
