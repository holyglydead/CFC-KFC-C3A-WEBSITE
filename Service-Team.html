The

Service Team

Couple Coordinators

Play an essential role in nurturing a supportive and spiritually enriching environment for the children within the ministry. They are typically married couples who have a strong commitment to faith and family, which they bring into their service within KFC. Acting as mentors, CCs provide guidance, support, and positive role modeling for the children and youth in the program.

ROCK Facilitators

ROCK (Reach Out Christ to Kids) Facilitators, often young leaders or former members of KFC themselves, guide these children in deepening their faith and understanding of Christ's teachings. They conduct activities, worship, and discussions designed to help children embody Christian values, build meaningful relationships, and develop a sense of responsibility to share their faith with others.

The Sector Heads

The Sector Heads are responsible for overseeing the ROCK program within their sector, which focuses on guiding children in their spiritual journey. They coordinate with ROCK Facilitators, plan and organize sector-wide events, and ensure that activities inspire children to live out the values of "Reaching Out Christ to Kids." They play a key role in preparing children for a smooth transition to the Youth for Christ ministry. Under the Sector are the Buhisan, Mambaling, San Lorenzo, and San Nicolas Chapters.

The Buhisan Chapter

The Buhisan Chapter, as part of Sector Three in Cebu, is situated in the picturesque, mountainous area of Cebu City. Despite its challenges, including the current lack of active Youth for Christ (YFC) members to support as ROCK leaders, the chapter remains committed to nurturing the faith of its young members through regular monthly Kids for Christ (KFC) sessions. The absence of a dedicated ROCK Leader has not deterred the chapter's dedicated Couple Coordinators and volunteers from organizing engaging and meaningful activities for the children.

The Mambaling Chapter

The Mambaling Chapter of Kids for Christ (KFC) in Cebu is an active and close-knit community within Couples for Christ (CFC) that is dedicated to providing a strong spiritual foundation for the children in the area. Located in a busy urban part of Cebu City, the Mambaling Chapter has become a hub of faith, friendship, and fun for kids, offering them a safe space to learn about Christ and grow in their faith amidst the bustling city environment.

The San Lorenzo Chapter

The San Lorenzo chapter specifically focuses on serving the children in the San Lorenzo community. It is part of the mission to "bring families back to the plan of God" by starting with the youngest members. The chapter offers a variety of activities, from fun games and arts and crafts to prayers and Bible stories. They focus on nurturing the children's faith, building a strong moral foundation, and helping them develop a sense of community and fellowship with other kids.

The San Nicolas Chapter

The San Nicolas chapter serves children and families within the San Nicolas community and organizes a variety of activities that encourage children to deepen their relationship with God, understand Catholic values, and connect with their peers in a meaningful, Christ-centered way. By creating a welcoming and inclusive environment, the San Nicolas chapter helps children experience a sense of belonging and purpose within their faith community.
